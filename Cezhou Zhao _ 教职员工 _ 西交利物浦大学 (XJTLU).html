    Profile

    Cezhou ZHAO (Ce Zhou ZHAO) is a professor at Xi'an Jiaotong- Liverpool University and a PhD supervisor at both University of Liverpool(UK) and Xi'an Jiaotong University (China).
    Prof. Zhao received his PhD degree in semiconductor physics and devices from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China, in 1995. He became a full professor in Dec. 1997 with Xidian University, China. He was a research associate/fellow at Liverpool University/Liverpool John Moores University, UK, from July 1998 to Sep. 2008.
    Dr. Zhao’s research activities involve reliability physics of VLSI technology, deposition and characterization of high-k materials, semiconductor integrated optics, GaN power electronic devices, Li ion batteries, carbon based supercapacitors, single molecule devices and solar cell technology.
    Prof. Zhao has authored or coauthored more than 250 publications, including 3 Chinese books, 1 English textbook and 150 journal papers. He also is the author or coauthor of more than 40 patents.
